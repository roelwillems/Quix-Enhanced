Quix Enhanced Quix on steroids

Your Quix bookmarklet on Steroids

Quix is an extensible bookmarklet made by Joost de Valk (Yoast), that allows you to easily access all your bookmarks and bookmarklets, across all your browsers, while maintaining them in only one spot.

By default Quix can be extended by loading it with your own custom commands file. Although it can be difficult to write your own custom file. With Quix Enhanced I'm incorporating the best custom commands (bookmarks and bookmarklets) in to a one of a kind custom Quix command file, free for you to use!

This file: "Quix Enhanced" is an extension of the basic Quix commands, and will incorporates many more commands and additional bookmarks/bookmarklets, easy to install and automatically updated!
